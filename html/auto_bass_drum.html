Overview

This project is a Business Intelligence & Visual Analytics review for a fictitious LLC, Mt Vernon Winery in Mt Vernon, IA.


Using only Tableau and Excel, it aims to establish an overall data exploration strategy followed by data manipulation and visualization. The visualizations aim to provide decision-making support to improve the winery’s business volume in 2022. I undertook this project after taking a class called Information Visualization as part of my Masters at the University of Iowa
